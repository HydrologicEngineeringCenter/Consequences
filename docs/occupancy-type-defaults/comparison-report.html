Methodology

  • Comparable ground: structure and contents depth-driven damage functions. go-consequences uses its depth driver (falling back to default, which mirrors it). Vehicle/other curves, erosion, salinity and wave drivers have no counterpart in the other source and are cataloged in the inventory instead.
  • Central values: charts and diffs use each ordinate's central estimate — Deterministic value, Triangular most-likely, Normal mean, Empirical histogram mean. HEC-FIA curve ordinates are deterministic except 16 curves carrying Normal standard deviations.
  • Uncertainty bands: shaded where a source has one — Triangular min→max, Normal ±2σ, Empirical min→max.
  • Diff metric: both curves evaluated on the union of their depth grids; outside a curve's range its endpoint value extends flat. Max/mean absolute difference is in percent-damage points.
  • Verdicts: Identical < 0.5 pts · Minor < 5 pts · Divergent ≥ 5 pts (max abs diff).
